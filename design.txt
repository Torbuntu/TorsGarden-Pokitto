Tor's Garden - Game Brainstorm

Loc  - There is a garden. The player plants various different plants in
 the garden.

why? - The garden starts pretty decrepit and beat up. The player takes the
 role as gardener to revive the garden to a better state. 

how? - Tending to the garden through Planting, Watering, Nurturing and by
protecting the plants from disease and insects (swater mini-game)

Obstacles - Insects will attempt to eat the plants. Tiers unlock when
a plant of the given tier reaches the "perfect" level. 

Win - To win, the player will revive the full garden, all tiers will be
completed and have at least 1 "perfect" plant. 

Extra - Achievments, mini-game score (insect battle).
